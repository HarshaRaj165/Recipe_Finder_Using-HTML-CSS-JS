Popcorn Recipe

Popcorn

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup popcorn kernels
  • 2-3 tablespoons oil (vegetable, coconut, or canola oil)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Butter (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Popcorn

  1. Heat 2-3 tablespoons of oil in a large, deep pot over medium heat. Make sure the oil covers the bottom of the pot.
  2. Add a few popcorn kernels to the oil. Once they pop, the oil is hot enough to add the rest of the kernels.
  3. Add the remaining popcorn kernels to the pot, cover it with a lid, and shake the pot gently to spread the kernels evenly.
  4. Let the popcorn pop, occasionally shaking the pot. Once the popping slows down (1-2 seconds between pops), remove the pot from the heat.

2. Seasoning the Popcorn

  1. Immediately transfer the popcorn to a large bowl.
  2. Sprinkle salt over the popcorn and toss to coat evenly.
  3. For buttered popcorn, melt butter and drizzle it over the popcorn while tossing.

3. Serving

  1. Serve the freshly popped popcorn in bowls and enjoy as a snack.
