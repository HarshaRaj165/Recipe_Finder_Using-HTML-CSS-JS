Cutlet Recipe

Cutlet

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup boiled mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1 tablespoon fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for shallow frying

For Coating

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Water, for making slurry

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Vegetable Filling

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add finely chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger garlic paste. Saut√© until the onions turn golden.
  3. Add turmeric powder, garam masala, and chaat masala. Mix well.
  4. Add the boiled mixed vegetables and mashed potatoes. Cook for 2-3 minutes, mashing and mixing them together.
  5. Turn off the heat, add chopped coriander leaves, and allow the mixture to cool.

2. Shaping the Cutlets

  1. Once the vegetable mixture is cool, shape it into small round or oval patties.

3. Preparing the Coating

  1. In a small bowl, make a slurry by mixing the all-purpose flour with water. The consistency should be thin enough for dipping.
  2. Dip each shaped patty into the slurry and then roll it in breadcrumbs until evenly coated.

4. Frying the Cutlets

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Shallow fry the cutlets until they are golden brown and crisp on both sides. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

5. Serving

  1. Serve hot vegetable cutlets with ketchup, mint chutney, or tamarind sauce.
