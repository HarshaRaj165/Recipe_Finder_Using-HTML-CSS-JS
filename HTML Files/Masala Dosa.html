Masala Dosa Recipe

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice (preferably parboiled)
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Water (for soaking and grinding)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil or ghee, for cooking the dosa

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately. Soak them in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked rice, dal, and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter. Add water as needed to achieve a flowing consistency.
  3. Transfer the batter into a large bowl, add salt, and mix well.
  4. Cover the batter and let it ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours until it rises and becomes slightly bubbly.
