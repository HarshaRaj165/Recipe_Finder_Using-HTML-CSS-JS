Sweetcorn Recipe

Sweetcorn

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sweet corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Cooking the Corn

  1. If using fresh corn, boil the corn kernels in water for 5-7 minutes until tender. If using frozen corn, you can steam or microwave them according to package instructions.
  2. Drain the water and transfer the cooked corn to a mixing bowl.

2. Seasoning the Corn

  1. While the corn is still hot, add butter and mix well until it melts and coats the corn.
  2. Add chaat masala, black pepper, red chili powder (if using), and salt. Mix well to evenly coat the corn with spices.
  3. Drizzle lemon juice over the corn and toss everything together.

3. Garnishing and Serving

  1. Transfer the sweet corn to serving bowls.
  2. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot as a snack or side dish.
