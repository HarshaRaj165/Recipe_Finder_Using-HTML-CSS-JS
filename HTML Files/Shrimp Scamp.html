Shrimp Scampi Recipe

Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients:

  • 500g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, chopped
  • 200g linguine
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the linguine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter and cook the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the white wine and lemon juice, and simmer for 2 more minutes.
  5. Toss the cooked linguine with the shrimp and sauce. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
