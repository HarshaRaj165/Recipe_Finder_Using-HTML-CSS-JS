Margherita Pizza Recipe

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly on top.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven, drizzle with olive oil, and top with fresh basil leaves.
  7. Season with salt and serve hot.
