Veg Fried Rice Recipe

Veg Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked basmati rice (cooled)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup capsicum (bell peppers), finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn (optional)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit (optional)
  • Spring onions, for garnish

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Rice

  1. Ensure the rice is cooked and completely cooled. Use leftover rice for best results, as fresh rice may turn sticky.

2. Stir-frying the Vegetables

  1. Heat oil in a large wok or pan over high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies (if using). Stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  4. Now add the carrots, capsicum, green peas, and sweet corn. Stir-fry on high heat for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crunchy.

3. Adding Seasoning

  1. Add soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper powder, and salt to taste. Stir well to combine.

4. Mixing the Rice

  1. Lower the heat and add the cooked rice to the stir-fried vegetables.
  2. Toss the rice gently with the vegetables and sauces, making sure it is evenly mixed without breaking the rice grains.
  3. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes on high heat to allow the flavors to combine.

5. Serving the Veg Fried Rice

  1. Turn off the heat and garnish the fried rice with chopped spring onions.
  2. Serve hot with your favorite Indo-Chinese side dish or enjoy it on its own.
