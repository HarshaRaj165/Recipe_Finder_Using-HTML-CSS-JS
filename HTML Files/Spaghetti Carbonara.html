Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Sea salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed

Instructions:

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to the boil and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a little oil until crispy, then add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated Pecorino and Parmesan cheeses.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pancetta and garlic mixture. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the hot pasta and stir vigorously to coat the spaghetti in the sauce. The heat of the pasta will cook the eggs.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
