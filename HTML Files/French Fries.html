French Fries

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 large potatoes
  • Oil, for deep frying
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper or seasoning of choice (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Potatoes

  1. Peel the potatoes and slice them into long, thin strips about 1/4 inch thick.
  2. Place the potato strips in a bowl of cold water and let them soak for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. After soaking, drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a clean kitchen towel.

2. Frying the Potatoes

  1. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, fry the potato strips in batches for 4-5 minutes until they are slightly golden but not fully crisp.
  3. Remove the fries and drain on paper towels. Let them cool down.
  4. After cooling, fry the potatoes again in hot oil for another 2-3 minutes until they are crispy and golden brown.
  5. Drain the fries on paper towels to remove excess oil.

3. Seasoning the Fries

  1. While the fries are still hot, sprinkle salt over them.
  2. You can also add pepper or any seasoning of your choice for extra flavor.

4. Serving

  1. Serve the hot, crispy French fries with ketchup, mayonnaise, or any dipping sauce of your choice.
