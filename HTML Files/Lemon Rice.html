Lemon Rice Recipe

Lemon Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked basmati rice (cooled)
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (split black lentils)
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 2 tablespoons peanuts
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Rice

  1. Ensure the rice is cooked and completely cooled. Use leftover rice for best results, as fresh rice may turn sticky.

2. Tempering (Tadka)

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok on medium heat.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chana dal and urad dal, and fry until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add peanuts, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute until the peanuts are roasted and the curry leaves become crispy.
  5. Add the green chilies and chopped onions (if using), and sauté until the onions turn soft and translucent.
  6. Sprinkle turmeric powder and mix well.

3. Mixing the Lemon Rice

  1. Lower the heat and add the cooked rice to the tempering.
  2. Add salt to taste and gently toss the rice to combine with the tempering mixture.
  3. Pour the freshly squeezed lemon juice over the rice and mix thoroughly.

4. Serving the Lemon Rice

  1. Turn off the heat and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  2. Serve hot with pickle, papad, or curd, and enjoy!
