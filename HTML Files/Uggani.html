Uggani Recipe

Uggani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped (optional)
  • 1 small potato, boiled and chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon roasted peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Lemon juice, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Puffed Rice

  1. Soak the puffed rice in water for 2-3 minutes until they soften.
  2. Drain the water and gently squeeze out any excess moisture from the puffed rice. Set aside.

2. Cooking the Uggani

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves and green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  5. If using, add chopped tomatoes and boiled potatoes. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  6. Sprinkle turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the drained puffed rice to the pan and mix gently to coat the puffed rice with the spices and vegetables.

3. Garnishing and Serving

  1. Add roasted peanuts and a squeeze of lemon juice. Mix gently.
  2. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  3. Serve Uggani hot, accompanied with mirchi bajji (fried green chilies) or chutney.
