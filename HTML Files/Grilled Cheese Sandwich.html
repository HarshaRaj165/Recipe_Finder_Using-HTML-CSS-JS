Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place a slice of cheese between two slices of bread, buttered side out.
  4. Grill the sandwich in the skillet until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted.
