Taco Salad Recipe

Taco Salad

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 taco seasoning packet
  • 4 cups lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • Tortilla chips for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan until browned. Add the taco seasoning and follow package instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, cherry tomatoes, shredded cheese, and cooked ground beef.
  3. Top with sour cream, salsa, and tortilla chips. Serve immediately.
