Shawarma

Ingredients:

For the Chicken Marinade

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 3 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt, to taste

For the Wraps

  • 4 large pita breads or flatbreads
  • 2 cups lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup cucumber slices
  • 1 cup tomato slices
  • Pickles (optional)

For the Garlic Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1. Marinating the Chicken

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, pepper, and salt.
  2. Add the chicken strips and mix well to coat evenly.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight, to marinate.

2. Cooking the Chicken

  1. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat and add a little oil.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken strips for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until fully cooked and slightly charred on the edges.
  3. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

3. Preparing the Garlic Sauce

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  2. Adjust seasoning to taste.

4. Assembling the Shawarma

  1. Warm the pita breads or flatbreads slightly on a skillet or in the oven.
  2. Spread a generous amount of garlic sauce over each flatbread.
  3. Top with cooked chicken strips, shredded lettuce, cucumber slices, tomato slices, and pickles (if using).
  4. Roll or fold the flatbread to enclose the filling.

5. Serving

  1. Cut the shawarma wraps in half and serve immediately with extra garlic sauce or hummus on the side.
