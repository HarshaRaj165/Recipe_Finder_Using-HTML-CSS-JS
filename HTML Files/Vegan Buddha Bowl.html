Vegan Buddha Bowl Recipe

Vegan Buddha Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 cup roasted chickpeas
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup roasted sweet potatoes
  • Tahini dressing

Instructions:

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, arrange the quinoa, chickpeas, mixed greens, avocado slices, and roasted sweet potatoes.
  3. Drizzle with tahini dressing and serve.
