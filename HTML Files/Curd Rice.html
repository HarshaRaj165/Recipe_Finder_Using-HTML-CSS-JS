Curd Rice Recipe

Curd Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cooled)
  • 1 1/2 cups plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional)
  • 1 small cucumber, finely chopped
  • 1 small carrot, grated (optional)
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (split black lentils)
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Curd Rice Base

  1. In a large mixing bowl, mash the cooked rice gently to break down any large lumps.
  2. Add plain yogurt and mix well. If the mixture is too thick, add a little milk to adjust the consistency.
  3. Add salt to taste and mix thoroughly.

2. Adding Vegetables (Optional)

  1. Add finely chopped cucumber, grated carrot, and green chilies (if using) to the rice mixture.
  2. Mix well to combine the vegetables with the curd rice.

3. Tempering (Tadka)

  1. Heat oil in a small pan over medium heat.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, chana dal, and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add dried red chilies, curry leaves, and a pinch of asafoetida (hing). Stir for a few seconds until fragrant.

4. Mixing the Tempering with the Curd Rice

  1. Pour the tempering over the curd rice mixture and gently mix to combine the flavors.

5. Serving the Curd Rice

  1. Garnish the curd rice with fresh coriander leaves.
  2. Serve chilled or at room temperature with a side of pickle, papad, or fried green chilies.
