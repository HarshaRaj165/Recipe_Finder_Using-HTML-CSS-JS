Upma Recipe

Upma

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (sooji/rava)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (optional)
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:

1. Roasting the Semolina

  1. Heat a dry pan over medium heat and add the semolina (sooji).
  2. Dry roast the semolina for 4-5 minutes, stirring continuously, until it turns light golden and fragrant.
  3. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.

2. Preparing the Tempering

  1. In the same pan, heat oil or ghee on medium heat.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, chana dal, and curry leaves. Sauté until the dals turn golden brown.
  4. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until the onions become translucent.
  5. Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.

3. Cooking the Upma

  1. Add 2 cups of water to the pan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Once the water starts boiling, lower the heat and gradually add the roasted semolina, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Keep stirring the upma until the semolina absorbs all the water and thickens.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 minutes until the upma becomes soft and fully cooked.

4. Garnishing and Serving

  1. Turn off the heat, add lemon juice (if using), and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  2. Serve hot upma with coconut chutney or pickle.
