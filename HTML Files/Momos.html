Momos Recipe

Momos

Ingredients:

For the Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water, as needed

For the Filling

  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, finely chopped (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the mixture to form a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.

2. Preparing the Filling

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the chopped garlic and onions until they are soft and translucent.
  2. Add the chopped cabbage, carrots, and soy sauce. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly cooked but still crunchy.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, and stir in the fresh coriander if using.
  4. Turn off the heat and let the filling cool.

3. Shaping the Momos

  1. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin circle about 3 inches in diameter.
  2. Place a small spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough circle.
  3. Fold the edges of the dough over the filling and pinch to seal the momo. You can pleat the edges to give it a traditional shape.

4. Steaming the Momos

  1. Heat water in a steamer and bring it to a boil.
  2. Place the momos in the steamer basket, making sure they are not touching each other.
  3. Steam the momos for 10-12 minutes until the dough becomes translucent and fully cooked.

5. Serving

  1. Serve the hot momos with a spicy dipping sauce or chutney.
