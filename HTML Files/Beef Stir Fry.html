Beef Stir Fry Recipe

Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef strips
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, marinate the beef with soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch.
  2. Heat a wok or large pan over high heat, then stir fry the beef until browned. Set aside.
  3. In the same wok, add the garlic, onions, bell pepper, and broccoli, and stir fry until tender.
  4. Return the beef to the wok, toss everything together, and cook for 2 more minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve over rice or noodles.
